Problem statement: Nutrition Label Accessibility

For the blind and visually impaired, nutrition labels are not only difficult to access, but also cumbersome to navigate in their current format even when they are able to be located and read through accessibility tools such as screen readers.

Affinity diagram: Nutrition Label Accessibility

The affinity diagram describes potential ways of accessing information, core features, accessibility considerations, different possible products, hurdles/problems, and finally some aspirational features.
